Bio

Zihao Yue is currently a PhD student at Renmin University of China (RUC), advised by Prof. Qin Jin. He received his B.E. degree in Computer Science from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2019. His research interests include language modeling and video understanding, with a focus on vision-and-language.
